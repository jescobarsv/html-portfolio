Must Watch Spider-Man Movies

How to fell in love with your friendly neighborhood Spider-Man

Spider-Man (2002)

The original movie, great all rounder popcorn flick!
9/10

Spider-Man 2 (2004)

The best one of the "Sam Raimi Trilogy", it builds and improves upon the first one.
10/10

Amazing Spider-Man 2 (2014)

Somewhat criticized but it's still a fun experience and has its own twist on the Green Goblin seen in the Raimi Trilogy.
7/10

Spider-Man Into the Spiderverse (2018)

Amazing fells short, a true masterpiece of animated comic book movies. A great time for all the family!
10/10

Spider-Man Homecoming (2017)

The first official Spider-Man movie made from Marvel Studios, quite entertaining and more grounded to the Comics.
8/10

Spider-Man No way Home (2022)

The one that ties it all in, having watched the rest this one hits way different!
10/10
